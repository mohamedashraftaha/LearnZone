LearnZone is a non-profit online educational platform that offers an exceptional expereince for highschool students where they can interact as if they are in an actual classroom !


Students can :

- Chat with their classmates and teachers -

- Recieve daily reminders on their school's calendar and book office hours -

- Watch live and recorded tutorials by their teachers-

- Access an online library with thousands of sources and books-

- Get updated of their class progress by viewing their gradebooks-

- Submit their assignments-


Teachers can :

- Upload assignments for their students -

- Send daily reminders through calendar -

- Conduct a live session or upload a recorded one -

- Upload students' grades -

- Chat with students and avoid the hassle of emails -


Parents can :

- Use their son's/daughter's account credentials to access their gradebook and stay updated with their progress in school -
